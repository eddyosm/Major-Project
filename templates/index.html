Overview

The ever-growing realm of deep learning offers a fascinating glimpse into the future of human-computer interaction. This project delves into one such application: automatic image caption generation. Our objective is to design a model that can bridge the gap between visual content and natural language descriptions. By harnessing the power of deep learning architectures, we aim to empower machines to "see" and interpret the world around them, and subsequently, express those observations in a human-understandable way.
This project centers around two fundamental deep learning models: Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks. Each plays a vital role in the image captioning process.
chevron_right Unveiling the Visual: Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
CNNs excel in the domain of image recognition and feature extraction. They function as the initial gateway for our image caption generator. When an image is presented, the CNN acts as a meticulous observer, dissecting the visual content pixel by pixel. Through a series of intricate convolutional and pooling layers, the CNN extracts crucial features from the image. These features may encompass objects depicted, their spatial relationships, dominant colors, and even textures. The extracted features essentially become a concise representation of the image's visual essence, ready to be interpreted by the next stage.
A LSTM-Based Neural Network Architecture
chevron_right Weaving the Narrative: Long Short-Term Memory (LSTMs)
LSTMs, a specific type of Recurrent Neural Network (RNN), are ideally suited for handling sequential data like sentences. In our project, the LSTM assumes the role of the storyteller. It receives the distilled visual representation extracted by the CNN and begins to craft a narrative. Unlike a traditional feedforward network, LSTMs possess a unique memory capability, allowing them to consider not only the current extracted features but also the previously generated words in the caption. This enables the LSTM to build a coherent and contextually relevant description, word by word.
